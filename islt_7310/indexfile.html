Home Page

Welcome! Thank you so much for taking time to visit my website. This website will be showcasing my work in my current career. I will be setting up the website to help organize and prepare for the writing of my dossier. As an employee of the University of Missouri Extension, faculty have the opportunity to apply for non-tenure track. The non-tenure track is based on the faculty member showing increasing excellence and achievement in the Core Competencies and Core Duties of Extension. Purpose: This website is to highlight and organize my work at the University of Missouri Extension. This is to help prepare for the writing of my dossier, which will allow me to apply for promotion. Goals and Objectives of Website: • Identify examples of work in my career that match with the Core Competencies and Core Duties of Extension • Well organized and functioning website • Easy to read and navigate website • Website is organized into parts that are required for the dossier
